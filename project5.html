BioRepublic
Brand Strategy & Visual identity

Category :
Beauty
Client :
BioRepublic
Start Date :
July 2022

The
Challenge

Biorepublic, a clean and sustainable skincare brand, wanted to solidify their position in the market. Despite their focus on quality and eco-friendly practices, they lacked a strong and consistent brand identity. This made it difficult for them to connect with potential customers and stand out amongst competitors.

The Solution

Biorepublic partnered with Unveiled Space to develop a clear strategy. They focused on creating a brand that offered high-quality yet simple skincare products for everyday use, at an accessible price point. They also developed a strong visual identity with a memorable electric blue color and a consistent typography system. Additionally, Biorepublic adopted a witty tone of voice, creating a unique brand personality that resonated with their target audience. This comprehensive rebranding helped Biorepublic establish themselves as a strong and consistent brand in the competitive skincare market.
