Easy Copy
Brand Strategy & Visual identity

Category :
Printing
Client :
Easy Copy
Start Date :
March 2021

The
Challenge

Stéphane Le Sech, founder of the PC Duo computer repair chain, recognized a challenge: simplifying the printing process. He envisioned a network of partner printers connected by a user-friendly online platform, eliminating the need for physical visits to printing shops. However, bringing this vision to life required expertise in branding and user experience (UX) design.

The Solution

Stéphane Le Sech, founder of PC Duo repair chain, recognized the challenge of simplifying printing. He envisioned a network of partner printers connected by a user-friendly online platform, eliminating physical shop visits. To achieve this, Stéphane partnered with US. We helped them by designing and developing the core website – allowing users to upload documents, choose printers, and track orders. Additionally, we crafted a strong visual identity for Easy Copy, including a stylized "E" and "C" logo and a bright color scheme. This visual identity would be used throughout the website and marketing materials, creating a consistent and recognizable brand experience. Ultimately, by cresating a user-friendly online platform and a strong visual identity, we helped Easy Copy achieve its vision of a streamlined and accessible printing solution for a wider audience.
